WOLIT’23 is jointly hosted by Institute of Data and Information (iDI) of Tsinghua Shenzhen International Graduate School, Institute of Network Coding (INC) of The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) and Shenzhen Research Institute of Big Data.

WOLIT aims to bring together the world’s leading researchers to explore deep unsolved problems in information theory, learning theory and the related fields.
